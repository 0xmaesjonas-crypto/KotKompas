The six people behind KotKompas

We're six students associated to the KU Leuven who built KotKompas as a project to make student life simpler, safer and more connected.

About KotKompas 🧭
Wat is KotKompas · Wat heb jij eraan
KotKompas
Student Hub · Leuven
"Everything a student needs in Leuven — school, horeca, social life, housing and safety — in one place."
The problem 🎯
What we set out to solve
📱
Too many platforms
Toledo, KU Loket, WhatsApp groups, Facebook events, random websites for housing — students juggle 10+ platforms every day.
🏙️
No local discovery
Students don't know what's happening around them — which bars have student deals tonight, which venues are busy, who's nearby.
🛡️
Safety is invisible
Students — especially in their first year — lack a simple, immediate way to reach help or a trusted friend when they don't feel safe.
